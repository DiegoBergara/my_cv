# contact-section (change delta)

## MODIFIED Requirements

### Requirement: Primary CTA prominence
The primary CTA (schedule a meeting) SHALL be visually dominant over the secondary CTA. Both CTAs SHALL be visually prominent and eye-catching (e.g. sufficient size, contrast, and hover/active states) so that they are clearly visible and invite interaction.

#### Scenario: Viewing both CTAs
- **WHEN** both CTAs are rendered in the contact section
- **THEN** the primary CTA SHALL use a filled/solid button style with accent color and high visibility, and the secondary CTA SHALL use an outlined/ghost button style that is also clearly visible and identifiable as a button

#### Scenario: CTA visibility
- **WHEN** the user views the contact section
- **THEN** both CTA buttons SHALL be immediately noticeable and clearly look clickable (e.g. through contrast, size, or subtle emphasis such as shadow or hover feedback)
